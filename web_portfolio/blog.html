Building my first Bootstrap site
I never knew responsiveness could be this easy. The grid system is a lifesaver — my Negros tourism site adapts to every screen size without custom media queries everywhere. Travel diaries: Negros Island
From the Ruins in Talisay to the sandbar in Manjuyod — this island has so much soul. The MassKara masks alone are worth the trip. I also discovered the best chicken inasal of my life at Manokan Country. If you ever visit the Philippines, put Negros on your list.
